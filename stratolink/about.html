The Team

Shepherd Kruse

Cofounder

Shepherd is a cadet at the US Air Force Academy. He is the head of the cadet rocketry team, and has extensive experience in hardware and electrical engineering. He has developed balloon payloads to test radiation shielding in the high stratosphere, rocket engines with responsive performance across atmospheric pressures, and multiple high-altitude telemetry and communication systems.

Shepherd has worked at Whittinghill Aerospace, and conducted research at the Weizmann Institute of Science.

Caleb Kruse

Cofounder

Caleb has a passion for applying emerging technologies into unexpected spaces. He studied oceanography at Stanford University with a thesis on studying and building a model of the physiological mechanisms of coral bleaching.

Caleb led the machine learning team at Leap Motion from 2017 until 2020, and has worked extensively in artificial intelligence and machine learning, virtual and augmented reality, and remote sensing and mapping.

He is also a National Geographic Explorer. With National Geographic, he has driven an ice cream truck across America and traced the saltwater aquarium fish trade in Indonesia.
